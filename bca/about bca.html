Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

ABOUT THE COURSE

Bachelor of computer application (BCA) is three years under graduate course spread over six semesters under self-financing scheme.

Eligibility

Candidates who have passed 10+2 examination from h.p.Board/CBCS/ICSE or any other examination considered equivalent to 10+2,by the himachal pradesh university,shimla -05, with 40% marks (35% marks for SC/ST category),shall be eligible.

Maximum age limit for admission to BCA course is 21 years for general category and 24 years for SC/ST category and 23 years for girl candidates
