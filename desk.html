I am happy to welcome you to this academic institution where you have come to pursue your higher studies. This institution has been a source of dream fulfilment for numerous aspirants coming from neighbouring areas who achieved their goals during the past 21 years. We believe that Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) under National Higher Education Mission will open up new arena of knowledge which will help you to choose any career of your interest. The courses offered in Community College include Advanced Diploma in Food Processing and Advanced Diploma Course in Pharmaceuticals.
