About SGGSJ Govt Degree College

With the beginning of the academic year 2019-20, SGGSJ Govt Degree College, Paonta Sahib has entered into its 25th Year of Academic excellence with a B++ NAAC grading which place it ahead of most of the other colleges in the state.Established in the year 1994,the college has earned its name far and wide for its academic and extracurricular excellence,It gets its name after the 10th Sikh Guru Shree Guru Govind Singh Jee who stayed here from 1685-1689 and founded the Khalsa Panth.


The college campus is spread over forty bighas in the vilage Shubh Khera of Paonta Sahib and 1.5 kilometers away from the Bus station. The college can be approached from the bus stand on foot within fifteen minutes.E-rickshaws also ply on the route.Nestled in a sugar mill in the early stages of its inception,the college has in the past years acquired a multistoried academic block with well-equipped labs,lecture halls,IT-Department and an air-Conditioned library along with the Administrative Block housing the NSS, Ranger & Rover units,IGNOU,Community College,Girls common room and a multipurpose hall. Work of remaining section of academic block has commenced.The college has the biggest playground in the town wherein number of sports activities can be witnessed throughout the day. The libarary has more then 9,700 books and e-access facility through N-LIST The college campus has wireless fifelity (wi-fi) system for online connecticity from every corner of the campus.


The college has been awarded financial support by Department of Biotechnology.Ministry of Science & Technology, Govt.of India under STAR colllege scheme to three Department viz. Botany,Chemistry & Zoology for three years.

The college is affiliated to Himachal Pradesh University,Shimla and recognized by the University Grants Commission, Government of India, New Delhi. Experienced and well-qualified dedicated faculty is a boon to students enrolled with this instution.The college also offers exposure to students through co-curricular activities for a multi-dimensional development of their personality.Students are encouraged to join NSS, Rangers and Rovers, Eco-club, Red Ribbon club, Science Socirty and Social Science Society according to their intrests.They hold various offices in the College Students Central Association (CSCA) through selection by merit.A well equipped Gymnasium and facilities for various games and sports are also available in the college.The college Magazine Bhanuja takes care of the creative talent and critical insight of the students on different issues. Various awards and prizes are given to meritorious student and outstanding sportsmen in the Anual Prize Distribution function,the chief awards being Gaurav and Daksh for overall achiever and sports person of outstanding caliber respectively.
