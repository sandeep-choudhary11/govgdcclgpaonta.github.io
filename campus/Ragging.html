The College Does Not Tolerate Ragging in Any Form

Ragging

"Ragging" means any act, conduct or practice by which dominant power of senior students, former students or outsiders,is brought to bear on students freshly enrolled or students who are in any way considered junior by other students and includes or collective acts or practices which:

01 Involve physical or psychological assault or threat or use of force or wrongful confinement or restraint.

02 Viloate the status,dignity and honour of such students.

03 Entil verbal abuse and aggression,indecent gestures and obscene behaviour.
Anti-Ragging

(1) No person shall practice ragging in any form, within or outside the premises of the institution.Ragging in and around the college campus or on road/approaches leading there to is strictly prohibited.If any student is found indulging in ragging,directly or indirectly,the college authorities shall be obliged under Rule 22,17(a), (b), (c) of H.P.University to expal the guilty student from the college.



(2) Any person who contravene the provisions of sub-section(1), shall, on conviction,be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to three years or with fine which may extend to Rs.fifty thousand rupees or with both.
